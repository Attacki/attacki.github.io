Daily Sentence Pattern

Published: 2017/09/1  at  03:00 PM

It is most helpful that I make these sentences by myself for me to learn English.

It is most helpful for me to learn English by making these sentences by myself.

Making these sentences on my own is the most effective way for me to learn English.

She want to read this book on her own.

Never before have I eaten such a delicious carrot.

Only in this way can we grow delicious carrot.

Only then did the rabbit meet the wolf.

all the child components should run their own logic respectly

You don’t love me, all you care about is the votes for the campaign.

The weather in this region is typically hot and humid.（这个地区的天气通常炎热潮湿。）

I’m very familiar with how to config project bundle tools. Such as webpack, cite, turbo, and others.

The economic aspect of the project is crucial for its success.（项目的经济方面对其成功至关重要。）

Her striking characteristic is her fearless attitude towards challenges.(面对挑战无所畏惧的态度，就是她引入注目的特点)

Have you seen a stray dog around here?

He provided a clear rationale for his decision to invest in the stock market.

The shepherd led his sheep up the dale to graze on the hillside.

His limp handshake gave away his lack of confidence. 他无力的握手暴漏了他缺乏信心。

The government’s legitimacy was questioned after allegations of electoral fraud. 在选举舞弊之后，政府的合法性受到了质疑。

The delegation from the company attended the trade fair to represent their products. 该公司的代表团参加了商品交易会，代表了他们的产品

The graph denotes the increase in sales over the past year.

The accuracy of the measurement depends on the precision of the instruments.

The frequency of the earthquakes in the region has increased significantly in recent years.（该地区的地震频率近年来显著增加。）

The formula for success is hard work and perseverance.（成功的准则是努力和坚持。）

If you…you must…
如果你要。。。。。你必须要。。。。。。

A detective is a very important person who is good at finding things.
我所说的侦探呢，他们都是非常厉害的人，能帮人找到掉了的东西。(侦探是个非常重要的人，擅长找东西。)

The detective can try and guess where Mr.Rabbit may be.
侦探小姐也许要试着猜猜兔子先生流落到哪了.
这句话还可以把里面的情态动词，can 和 may 都换成 could 和 might，这样可以显得更委婉，与时态没有关系 The detective could try and guess where Mr.Rabbit might be.

have sb do…
让某人做…

I’ll have my brother call you.
I‘ll have my brother give you a call.

No, I do love you.
强调一下 love 这个词，就有一种我确实爱你的那种感觉。

I wonder if I should wear a coat.（我想是不是啥啥啥，可以说 I wonder if…）
我在想我是不是该穿个外套。（if 放在 wonder 后面表达的意思是”是不是“ if 放在 think 后面表达的意思是”如果“）
